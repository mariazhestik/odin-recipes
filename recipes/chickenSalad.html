Best Chicken Salad

Description

This chicken salad recipe is the best and a family favorite! I like to use leftover
roast chicken or baked chicken breasts that have been sprinkled with basil or
rosemary.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Mayonnaise
  • Celery
  • Almonds

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Stir them all in a bowl until well combined.
  3. Mix in chicken. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Enjoy!
