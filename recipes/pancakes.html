Banana pancakes

Description

Crowd-pleasing banana pancakes made from scratch that are ready in minutes.
A fun twist on ordinary pancakes.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • An Egg
  • Milk
  • Vegetable oil
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Combine your dry ingredients (flour, sugar, salt, baking powder) in one bowl and
    your wet ingredients (egg, milk, vegetable oil, mashed bananas) in another
    bowl.
  2. Add the dry ingredients to the bowl with the wet ingredients, then stir until
    they're incorporated. It's OK if your batter is slightly lumpy.
  3. Pour the batter in Â¼ cup portions onto a lightly oiled pan or griddle over
    medium-high heat.
  4. Cook for a few minutes, flip with a spatula, and cook for another few minutes
    (or until each side is golden brown).
  5. Serve your banana pancakes immediately. They're delicious alone or with your
    favorite pancake toppings.
